## What is an Artisan Keycap?

An artisan keycap is a custom, handcrafted keycap designed to replace a standard key on a mechanical keyboard (usually the Escape key, but sometimes the Spacebar or other modifier keys). Unlike mass-produced keycap sets, artisans are created in small batches by independent makers and artists.

Think of them as miniature sculptures that live on your desk. They serve as a focal point for your keyboard's aesthetic, adding a touch of personality, storytelling, and warmth to an otherwise industrial object.

## How Are Artisan Keycaps Made?

The process of creating a handmade keycap is a labor of love that requires patience, precision, and artistic vision. While different makers use different techniques, here at [Oai Studio](https://oai.studio), our process involves several meticulous steps:

### 1. The Concept and Sketch
Every tiny companion starts as an idea. We draw inspiration from everyday life in Kyoto—a soft piece of toast, a sweet strawberry pudding, or a quiet afternoon in a cafe. These concepts are sketched out on paper before moving to the digital realm.

### 2. 3D Sculpting and Printing
Once the design is finalized, we sculpt the character in 3D software. This allows us to ensure the stem perfectly fits Cherry MX switches. We then use high-resolution, full-color 3D resin printers to bring the digital sculpture into the physical world. This technology allows us to capture incredibly fine details, like the texture of a waffle or the sprinkles on a donut.

### 3. Hand-Finishing
This is where the magic happens. After printing, each keycap is carefully cleaned, cured, and inspected. Our studio artisans then apply hand-painted details and a specialized protective coating. This transparent finish not only gives the keycap a beautiful, glossy look but also protects it from the wear and tear of daily typing. 

Because of this hand-finishing process, no two Oai Studio keycaps are exactly alike. Each one is a unique piece of functional art.

## How to Choose Your First Artisan Keycap

If you’re looking to buy your first custom keycap, here are a few things to consider:

*   **Switch Compatibility:** The most important factor! Make sure the artisan is compatible with your keyboard's switches. Most modern artisans, including all [Oai Studio collections](https://oai.studio/collections/artisan-keycaps), are designed for Cherry MX cross-stem switches (which also include Gateron, Kailh, and Outemu).
*   **Profile and Placement:** Consider where you want to put the keycap. Most artisans are designed for the Esc key (1U size) and feature a profile similar to Cherry or OEM row 1. If you want to replace a Backspace or Enter key, you'll need specific sizes like 2U or 6.25U.
*   **Aesthetic and Theme:** Does your desk setup lean towards minimalist, cyberpunk, or kawaii? Choose a companion that fits your vibe. For example, our [Cream Bear Series](https://oai.studio/collections/frontpage) is perfect for adding a soft, kawaii touch to pastel or light-colored keyboards.
