```markdown
[IC] Oai Studio — Cream Bear Artisan Keycap Series | Kyoto, Japan

Hello Geekhack community!

I'm WuVivi, the founder of Oai Studio — a small artisan keycap studio 
based in Kyoto, Japan.

**About the Cream Bear Series:**
The Cream Bear is a tiny companion designed to bring warmth to your 
everyday keyboard experience. Each piece is:
- Full-color 3D printed with eco-friendly resin
- Hand-finished with transparent protective coating
- Compatible with Cherry MX cross-stem switches
- Available in multiple colorways

**Current lineup:**
- Toast Cream Bear — $39
- Pudding Boat Cream Bear — $49
- Picnic Basket Cream Bear — $49
- [+ 5 more colorways]

**Where to buy:**
https://oai.studio/collections/artisan-keycaps

**Social:**
- Instagram: @theoaistudio
- Twitter: @studiooai

Looking forward to your feedback!
```
